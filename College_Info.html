About PCCOE
Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering

Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE), Pune, is one of the leading engineering institutions under Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET). The institute is known for academic excellence, industry-oriented education, and strong placement records.

Vision
Institutional Goal

To be a leading engineering institute that produces globally competent professionals with strong ethical values, innovation skills, and leadership qualities.

Give students opportunities to develop leadership skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities.

Mission
Our Commitments

• Provide quality technical education
• Encourage research & innovation
• Strengthen industry collaboration
• Promote overall student development
• Give students global exposure
• Foster ethical values and social responsibility

Academic Programs
UG & PG Courses

• Computer Engineering
• Information Technology
• Electronics & Telecommunication
• Mechanical Engineering
• Civil Engineering
• Electrical Engineering
• Artificial Intelligence & Data Science
• Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
• Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Placements
Career Opportunities

The Training & Placement Cell at PCCOE provides excellent placement opportunities through top recruiters, industry training, and skill development programs.

Includes companies like TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Capgemini, Accenture, L&T Infotech, Tech Mahindra, IBM, and many more.
